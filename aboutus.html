About the Book:

This book offers a layered and structured exploration of the human digestive system, presenting anatomical details progressively through multiple levels.

Each layer reveals a specific degree of anatomical complexity, allowing students to build their understanding step by step. This gradual approach facilitates clearer and more accurate visualization of the digestive structures.

To enhance the learning experience, the book is accompanied by an interactive web page that allows users to display or hide different anatomical layers as needed. This interactive feature makes the study of anatomy more engaging, accessible, and effective.
